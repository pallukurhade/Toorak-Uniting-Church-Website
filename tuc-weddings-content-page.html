Making the arrangements for your wedding

Toorak Uniting welcomes couples considering marrying in our historic and beautiful church in the context of a Christian service.

It is very important to book the Church as early as possible to ensure the availability of the date and time you require. A provisional booking can be made by ringing the church office on (03) 9829 0300, Monday-Friday and a booking form will be mailed to you together with a 'Wedding Information Guidelines' leaflet. A $50.00 non refundable deposit will be required to confirm the booking.

Marriage Service follows the Uniting Church format

Some variation in the content of the Service may be allowed. This Church is open to marriages of mixed religion and although clergy from another denomination cannot conduct the marriage within the Uniting Church it may be negotiated that they assist with or bless the marriage.

Flowers and confetti

We do not provide flowers but can recommend florists in the area. Vases and pedestals are available on request.No confetti may be thrown in the Church grounds.

Parking

There is room for 4 bridal cars in the Church grounds. Ample parking on Toorak Road and on the side streets.Special arrangements can be made for people with disabilities.
